# Customer Behavior Analysis

## Project Overview
This project focuses on analyzing retail customer behavior and shopping trends to help a business understand purchasing patterns, customer segments, and sales performance.

The analysis follows a real-world data analytics workflow, starting from raw data and ending with business-ready insights and dashboards.

## Problem Statement
Retail companies collect large volumes of customer and transaction data but often lack clarity on which factors truly influence purchasing behavior and repeat sales. 

This project aims to analyze customer shopping data to identify trends across demographics, product categories, sales channels, and purchasing preferences. The objective is to generate actionable insights that can support data-driven decisions in marketing strategy, customer engagement, and product optimization.

## Key Business Questions
- Which customer segments contribute the most to revenue?
- How does purchasing behavior differ across age groups and genders?
- What impact do discounts, seasons, and payment methods have on sales?
- Which factors are associated with repeat purchases?
